Who is Roselia?

Roselia is your old friends that always give abdul Mie Setan

Roselia is an all-girl band in the BanG Dream! franchise. The group consists of five members, namely Minato Yukina on vocals, Hikawa Sayo on guitar, Udagawa Ako on drums, Shirokane Rinko on the keyboard, and Imai Lisa on bass. They debuted with the song BLACK SHOUT. Their band name was coined from combining the words Rose and Camellia. They're a popular gothic J-Rock band that is recognized at a near-professional level and is catching the eyes of the music industry and scouters.
